TEA@UCSD's 2023 Halloween Maze

About

OPERAHOUSE is Themed Entertainment Association at UCSD's 4th annual Halloween horror maze, in collaboration with Tritonfest. This student designed/run horror maze will be located on campus and free for all UCSD students in Winter 2023. Check out our YouTube channel above to see our previous haunts!

Registration

Event registration is live now! Sign up yourself and your group for one of our Friday Feb 17th or Saturday Feb 18th dates here! Registration fills up fast - don't wait!

Involvement

Sign up to scare act (audition will be held for "lead" role only) by filling out our interest form.

Auditions: Once you have filled out the interest form, you will receive an email with all relevant information, including the Dance Studio waiver, the waiver dropbox, the Audition time slot sign-up , and the Audition Intake Form. Don't forget to join our Discord server for updates and more!

We are also recruiting operational volunteers (bag check, ushers, etc.). Sign up here!

Recruitment for both scare actors and volunteers continues until the last day of the event! We'd love to see you there.
